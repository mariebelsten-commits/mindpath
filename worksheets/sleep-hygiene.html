Sleep Hygiene

Sleep is essential for mental health. While needs vary, 7–9 hours is commonly recommended. Poor sleep can raise anxiety, lower mood, and affect focus and judgment. Struggling with sleep? Use the tips below to build better habits.

😴 Why Sleep Matters

Good sleep steadies mood, sharpens focus, and lowers anxiety. Small, consistent habits make the biggest difference over time.
