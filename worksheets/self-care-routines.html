Looking After Yourself

Self-care means doing the things you enjoy and taking deliberate steps to support your physical, emotional, mental, social, and spiritual wellbeing. Having a clear plan makes it easier to practise self-care regularly — not just when you're already stressed. This worksheet helps you build a personalised self-care menu and commit to small, realistic actions across the week.

Work through each step below in turn. Use your self-care menu to inspire your weekly plan — small and consistent always beats ambitious and unsustainable.

📋 Check In With Yourself

Start with a short self-assessment to see how well you're currently looking after yourself.

Build My Self-Care Menu

Write down activities you can turn to when you need rest, balance, or energy. 🌿 Remember: You don't need to do something different every day — repeating activities that work is perfectly fine. The goal is consistency, not variety. Even on difficult days, one small act of self-care sends an important message to yourself: I am worth looking after.
