My Safety Plan & Support Network

A practical tool to guide you through moments of crisis — completed in advance so it's ready when you need it most.

🆘 If you are in immediate danger, please call 999 (UK) or your local emergency number now.

🛡️ What Is a Safety Plan?

A personalised set of coping strategies and contacts you prepare in advance to help you stay safe during periods of emotional crisis.

📋 How to Use It

Fill in each section honestly and thoughtfully. Keep your completed plan somewhere accessible — you might also share it with someone you trust.

🌐 Support Network Map

The second part helps you identify your wider network — people, professionals, and everyday sources of support that can help you through difficult times.
