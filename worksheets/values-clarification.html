Values Clarification

Values are the things that matter most to you and guide how you want to live. Unlike goals — which can be completed — values are ongoing directions. Clarifying your values helps you make choices that feel meaningful and stay motivated through challenges.

Values vs Goals

Goals can be achieved and ticked off. Values are like a compass — they point the way and guide every step, no matter where you are in life.

Why It Matters

Acting in line with your values rebuilds purpose and self-respect, even when your mood is low or motivation feels distant.

Taking Action

Small, values-aligned actions create momentum. Each step teaches your brain that meaningful change is possible.

How to use this worksheet: Work through each step in order. Start by selecting the values that resonate most with you, then narrow them to your top five. Define what each value means to you personally, plan one small action for each, and finish with a brief reflection.
