⏸️ Timeout Technique

A structured pause to interrupt escalation, reduce emotional intensity, and regain control before responding.

Step 1 — Notice escalation

Recognise early signs (thoughts, body sensations, urges).

Step 2 — Step away

Physically or mentally remove yourself from the trigger.

Step 3 — Regulate

Use breathing, grounding, or distraction to reduce arousal.

Step 4 — Return intentionally

Re-engage only when intensity has reduced.
