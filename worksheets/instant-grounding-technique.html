Instant Grounding Technique

For moments when everything feels overwhelming. This gentle exercise helps you feel a little more connected, settled, and safe in the present moment.

🌿 What Is Grounding?

Grounding techniques help you reconnect with the present moment when you feel anxious, distressed, numb, or disconnected.

🔢 The 3-2-1 Method

By noticing 3 things you can see, 2 you can hear, and 1 you can feel, you gently redirect your attention from distress back to your surroundings.

💾 Save Your Practice

Complete the exercise and save your entry — a helpful reminder of what worked for you that you can return to whenever you need it.

🌬️ Pause for a Moment

If you can, take one slow, gentle breath.

There is no need to change or control your breathing. Simply notice it as it is.

🧘 Notice Your Body

If possible, place both feet firmly on the floor. Take a moment to notice the support beneath you and the weight of your body being held.

What sensations can you notice in your body right now? (For example: tension, heaviness, warmth, tingling, numbness.)
