Probability Estimation

Anxiety often overestimates how likely a feared outcome really is. This exercise helps you identify a worry, examine the actual evidence, and calculate a fairer, more realistic estimate.

🎯 Name the Fear

Identify a specific feared outcome and rate how likely it feels right now, before examining any evidence.

⚖️ Weigh the Evidence

List the real evidence for and against the outcome — focusing on facts rather than how the worry feels.

📉 Revise the Estimate

Re-rate the likelihood with the evidence in front of you. Fears often shrink once you see the facts clearly.
