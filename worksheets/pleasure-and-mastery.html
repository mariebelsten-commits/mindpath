Pleasure & Mastery

Scheduling enjoyable activities is one of the most effective ways to lift your mood and rebuild momentum

What is Pleasure & Mastery?

Activities that bring enjoyment (pleasure) or a sense of achievement (mastery) are powerful tools for improving mood — especially when depression makes it hard to get started.

How to Use This List

Browse the five activity categories below. Click once to mark something for this week (green), click again to schedule it for the next few months (amber), click a third time to unmark it.

Why It Works

When we feel low we naturally withdraw — which deepens the slump. Deliberately scheduling small pleasures interrupts this cycle, creates positive experiences, and builds upward momentum.
